DNA Complement by Aohan Dang

Description
Enter the nitrogenous bases of a strand of DNA and find the sequence of bases on its
complementary strand.

How to install and run
Send dna.89f to your TI-89 or TI-89 Titanium. The correct syntax is dna("base sequence").
Use quotation marks around the sequence of bases, which should be capital letters of
A, T, C, and G.
