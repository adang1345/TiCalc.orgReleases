Hangman 2P 1.0 by Aohan Dang

Description
Play Hangman against a friend.

How to install and run
Send hangman.89p to your TI-89 or TI-89 Titanium. Type hangman() on the home screen and press
ENTER. At the end of each game, you must enter the whole word by pushing F2, even when the whole
word is revealed by guessing letters.
